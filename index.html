2025 HDSI Capstone Showcase

Poster session for the undergraduate data science major's senior capstone program

Friday, March 14th, 2025, 11AM-2:30PM

Price Center East Ballroom, UC San Diego Campus


The event is split into two sessions – Session 1, from 11AM-12:30PM, and Session 2, from 1-2:30PM. Each project will only be presented in one session.

With questions, contact Umesh Bellur at ubellur@ucsd.edu.

Registration is now open. Click HERE to register

This year, we have 72 project groups spread across a variety of topics. The showcase is divided into two blocks based on topics, 11AM-12:30PM and 1-2:30PM. The showcase will operate as a poster session, where attendees can approach the projects that interest you and ask their teams questions.

Below, you'll find the posters, websites, reports, and code repositories for all projects.
